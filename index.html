My Background / About Me
I've been working with GIS for 18 years, in various capacities; I've worked for local governments, nonprofit groups, and private consultants to provide GIS services for a diverse range of clients. I have a BA in Urban Studies from Trinity University, a Masters of Urban Planning and Policy degree from the University of Illinois at Chicago, and an MS in GIS Administration from the University of West Florida. Throughout my career, I keep coming back to GIS because of my passion for geospatial work and the powerful (and underutilized) ways in which it can be used to examine data in a different light.
I am looking for part time/contract GIS work that can be performed remotely. I excel in working with Python, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL. I also have significant experience with PostgreSQL, Microsoft SQL Server, PostGIS, and GeoJSON. I have been an ESRI user since 2007, and am also quite comfortable with QGIS. If you have extra GIS work that needs to be done, reach out to me via e-mail at kyle.mingus@gmail.com or throw some time on my calendar here.
